Biography

Volbeat is a Danish heavy metal band formed in Copenhagen in 2001. They are inspired by classic rock and roll artists such as Elvis Presley and Johnny Cash. Their current line-up consists of vocalist and guitarist Michael Poulsen, guitarist Rob Caggiano, drummer Jon Larsen and bassist Kaspar Boye Larsen. The band is signed to Dutch label Mascot Records and has released six studio albums. All of their studio albums have been certified gold in Denmark while their second album "Rock the Rebel/Metal the Devil" received platinum status, and their 2010 release "Beyond Hell/Above Heaven" was subject to widespread international critical acclaim, receiving double platinum in Denmark, platinum in Finland and Germany, and gold in the United States, Sweden, and Austria.
